Profile Test

This is a litle test to help you reflect about your prefered positions at the time of working with people.

It is composed of questions regaring your practice that add up to shape a profile. You can review previous tests and see your scores to have a more nuanced sense.

The idea is for it to be flexible, and for it to be representative of yourself. I hope it can help you as a mirror that keeps track of your process.

As a reference you can use the bellow numbers.

  • Bellow 40% is desagreement
  • Between 40% and 60% its neutral
  • Between 60% and 80% its a clear preference
  • Between 80% and 100% its your prefered view
